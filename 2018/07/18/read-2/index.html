Local Low-Rank Matrix Approximation with Preference Selection of Anchor Points

Motivation

  • In LLORMA, a critical step is to randomly select anchor points for each local matrix. This anchor-point selection method may lead to a bad performance and low efficiency because the anchor-points may be in the sparse region or in the overlapping area.

Contribution

  • propose a preferable anchor-point selecting method with Clustering for LLORMA . The method improves both the effectiveness and efficiency of local low-rank matrix factorization.
